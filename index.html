FAQ

Quick Answers

How long does it take to set up? ▼
Less than 30 minutes. We connect your dedicated mailbox, and you start forwarding exception emails.
Do I need a credit card for the trial? ▼
No. The 1‑month free trial requires no credit card and no commitment.
How is my data protected? ▼
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES‑256). We are fully compliant with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019. We process only the subject and body of exception emails — no attachments, no contact lists. We can also sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) to meet your compliance requirements.
Do you integrate with KRA eTIMS? ▼
Yes. We are actively exploring integration with KRA eTIMS to enable automatic tax invoice generation and compliance reporting. For enterprise clients, we can accelerate custom integration tailored to your existing invoicing system.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel anytime without penalty. No hidden fees.
What if I exceed my monthly exceptions? ▼
We'll automatically upgrade you to the next tier or offer a custom plan based on your volume.
